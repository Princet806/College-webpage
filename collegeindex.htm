About Us

Meerut Institute of Technology (MIT), established in 2007, is one of the top engineering and professional education institutions in Uttar Pradesh, India. It offers a variety of undergraduate and postgraduate programs and is affiliated with Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam Technical University, approved by AICTE. The campus provides state-of-the-art facilities including hostels, sports facilities, libraries, and strong placement support.

Academic Programs

B.tech

Bachelor of Technology Our B.Tech program offers undergraduate and graduate studies in various engineering disciplines, such as Computer Science, Electrical, Mechanical, and Civil Engineering. The program emphasizes innovation, problem-solving, and real-world applications.

B.Pharma

Bachelor of Pharmacy The B.Pharma program cultivates skilled professionals for the pharmaceutical and healthcare industries. Students learn about drug development, medicinal chemistry, and pharmacology, with hands-on training in labs and hospitals. Graduates are prepared for careers as pharmacists, researchers, and healthcare specialists.

BCA

Bachelor of Computer Applications Our BCA program focuses on developing strong foundations in computer science, programming, and information technology. The curriculum includes courses on software development, data structures, and networking. Coupled with an optional MBA pathway, the program also offers business management skills for leadership roles in the tech industry.

BBA

Bachelor of Business Administration The BBA program provides a foundation in business management, economics, finance, and marketing. Through internships and case studies, students gain real-world corporate exposure. The program also offers an MBA track, helping students prepare for leadership roles in finance, marketing, and other industries.
