Facilities

We provide the best facilities in all the areas of the college to ensure a comfortable and conducive learning environment for our students. explore the facilities we offer.

Hostel

Our hostels provide a comfortable and secure living environment for students. With modern amenities and a friendly atmosphere, our hostels are a home away from home.

Library

Our library is a treasure trove of knowledge, with a vast collection of books, journals, and digital resources. It is a quiet and serene space for students to study and research.

Labs

Our labs are equipped with the latest technology and equipment to provide students with hands-on experience and practical learning. From computer labs to science labs, we have it all.

Sports

We believe in the holistic development of our students, and sports play a vital role in that. Our sports facilities include a gym, playgrounds, and courts for various sports activities.

Canteen

Our canteen offers a variety of delicious and nutritious food options for students and staff. It is a popular spot on campus for meals and snacks.

Auditorium

Our state-of-the-art auditorium is equipped with modern audio-visual facilities and can accommodate a large audience. It is the venue for various events, seminars, and cultural activities.

Transport

We provide transport facilities for students and staff, ensuring a safe and convenient commute to and from the campus. Our buses are well-maintained and follow a regular schedule.

Medical

Health and well-being are a top priority for us, and we have a well-equipped medical center on campus. Our qualified medical staff provide round-the-clock care and support to students and staff.

Wifi

We understand the importance of staying connected in today's digital age. That's why we provide high-speed wifi connectivity across the campus, ensuring that students and staff can access information and resources seamlessly.

Security

The safety and security of our students and staff are of utmost importance to us. We have a dedicated security team that ensures a safe and secure environment on campus at all times.
